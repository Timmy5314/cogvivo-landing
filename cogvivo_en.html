// Origin

This didn't start
as a company.

It began as frustration. Three years of deep research into what the science actually says about cognitive performance — not the marketing version, but the primary literature. Every claimed mechanism verified. Every dose compared to the human trials the claim rests on. Every supplement on the market tested against the studies manufacturers cite themselves.

The conclusion was always the same: the products contain a fraction of the amounts that produce effects in those studies. Not by mistake. By design. More active substance costs more money, and the average consumer doesn't check the label anyway. That model works commercially. It doesn't work neurologically.

Cogvivo Protocol exists because there had to be a formulation without that compromise. Every mechanism covered. Every dose clinically active. Every compound transparent on the label, verifiable through the studies behind it. If you're reading this and thinking "finally" — this product is for you.
